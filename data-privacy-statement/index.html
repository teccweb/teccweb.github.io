Data Privacy Statement

The types of personal data we may collect include your name, contact information, payment details, and any other information necessary for the provision of our products and services.

  • To communicate with you regarding your orders, inquiries, or any other relevant information
  • To provide customer support and address any issues or concerns you may have

  • To comply with legal obligations or defend our legal rights

We will ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your personal data when it is transferred internationally.
